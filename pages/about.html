The Energy Renaissance

Founder's Narrative

Chigozie Moses Ezeobidi

"Energy is not just a utility; it is the bedrock of dignity and industrial sovereignty for West Africa."

With over two decades of experience in renewable energy and security solutions, Romlue Energy was founded with a singular mission: to provide reliable solar power and advanced CCTV security systems across Nigeria, transforming how communities and businesses access clean energy and protection.

Under his direction, the company has evolved from a consultancy into a powerhouse of renewable infrastructure. His philosophy integrates cutting-edge German engineering standards with deep localized knowledge of the Nigerian landscape, ensuring projects are both world-class and context-specific.

Our Ethics

Corporate Philosophy

Technical Excellence

We adhere to uncompromising engineering standards. Every installation undergoes rigorous stress-testing and quality assurance protocols based on international renewable energy certifications (IEC). For us, reliability is the only metric of success.

Social Responsibility

A core mandate of our operations is the upliftment of displaced communities. We prioritize the installation of micro-grids in IDP camps and rural outposts, ensuring that energy access serves as a catalyst for social reintegration and safety.
